History of CIMAGE

CIMAGE, inaugurated by the Hon C.M. of Bihar, Mr. Nitish Kumar in 2009, has come true to the high expectation of its stakeholders, namely students, Guardians, Academicians and mass at large and achieved phenomenal success.

One of the unique things, which makes itself stand apart is the innovative Teaching Pedagogy, followed at CIMAGE. The ‘Learning by Doing’, ‘Case Study Teaching’, Unique Assessment (Exam) System, which not only judges the students through their ‘Written Answers’ but examines the students in a very comprehensive manner, including their Skills, Personality, Project, Presentations, Learning, other Human Capabilities and Traits, needed to be successful in life. No wonder, CIMAGEians have set an example for others to follow by grabbing the title of being University Topper, year after years. Since inception, CIMAGEians have been topping University Examinations and winning Gold Medals. That is the reason, CIMAGEians find themselves very competent and confident in dealing with the Job responsibilities and challenges of life.

CIMAGE's achievements have been acknowledged nationwide and a number of awards have been conferred upon: Getting awarded as ‘Atal Shresth Sansthan Samman’ for its Extra-ordinary contributions and achievements in the field of Management and IT Education in BIhar by the Union HRD Minister Sri Ramesh Pokhariyal Nishank’ or ‘Most Emerging Institute for Management Education-2012’, on national level by the All India Management Education Association (AIMA) or ‘Best B-School of India (East) Award by ASSOCHAM, Association of Indian Universities & Ministry of HRD, Government of India by Dr. Ram Shankar Katheria, Hon. Minister of Education for State’ are some of the many awards and acclaims, which CIMMAGE has been rewarded with.
